# AI Skills College Graduates Need to Get Hired Faster

## Introduction
Graduating college is exciting—but entering today’s job market can feel overwhelming. Employers aren’t just looking for degrees; they want candidates who can solve problems and use modern tools effectively.

---

## Key Sections

### Practical AI Skills Over Theory
Employers value graduates who can apply AI tools to real tasks, not just explain concepts.

### Using ChatGPT to Increase Productivity
ChatGPT can assist with research, writing, summarization, and planning—helping graduates work faster and smarter.

### Building a Digital Portfolio with GitHub
Documenting AI-assisted projects on GitHub shows initiative and practical skill.

### Real-World Applications Employers Value
From content creation to workflow automation, AI skills help graduates demonstrate immediate impact.

---

## Conclusion / Call to Action
Graduates who combine foundational knowledge with hands-on AI tools stand out faster. Start small, document your work, and show employers what you can do.
